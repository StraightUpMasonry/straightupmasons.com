Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do anything decorative to mix up the monotony of a flat block wall?

We can do different variations like:
-Split face course
-Lattice cap block
-It really just depends on whether you want to have what your neighbors have or want to stick out a little bit.

Do you do wrought iron gates or will I need to get those somewhere else?

We have a welding/fabrication division of our company dedicated to creating:
Custom gates
Deck rails
Spiral decks
Etc.
Pretty much anything that requires metal fabrication.

What if I want to border my yard without messing with my view?

A popular choice for many of our customers (especially in the higher end sub divisions) is 4’ of block wall with 2’ of iron on top.

What is the best way to get a hold of you?

I always have my cell phone on me, I compulsively answer it and I check my emails once or twice a day. So between the two of those you’ll be able to get hold of me no matter what.

How long does it usually take to get a quote?

I can usually have a quote to you within 48 hours of your application, depending on what I have going on that day and how close you are to where I’m working.

Do you do commercial jobs?

We do small commercial projects, but we try to keep it 20,000 Feet and under.
