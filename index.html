1. Quote

We’ll contact you within 24 hours of applying for a quote to set up a time when we can drop by and take measurements. Quoting a project usually takes under 30 minutes and we’ll give you a price, go over possible timelines and discuss any special requirements for your job. We'll also send you an official estimate via email after we leave.

2. Course Work

The first thing we do when starting a project is check with Blue Stakes to make sure we don’t dig up any underground power, gas or anything else. Then we bring in the Mini-X to dig your footings followed by a compactor to make sure you have a solid foundation. Next we pour concrete, including plenty of rebar and finally put in the first course of block into the wet concrete.

3. Lay Block

This is where we bring in the noise (figuratively speaking) me and the boys come in and throw your wall up. If we have a project wet, set and stalked there aren't many residential walls we can’t put all the way up in a day or two.
